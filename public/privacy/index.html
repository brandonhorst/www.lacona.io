Lacona App and lacona.io Privacy Policy

The Information you Provide to Us

We take your privacy seriously and, when you provide us with any personal information, we endeavour to keep it safe. We will not share your personal information with third-parties and will take all reasonable precautions to protect it.

We do not control nor take any responsibility for the code that runs in User Addons - please see the privacy policy of the individual User Addon in question.

Tracking and cookies

To help us understand how we can make the Lacona website better for you, we use a Google Analytics tracking code on lacona.io. This information is anonymous and does not provide us with identifiable information.

The Lacona app does not currently track, but reserves the right to track anonymized, high-level usage metrics. Individual commands, inputs, queries, or results from using Lacona are not measured.

Personal Data

Aside from the aforementioned metrics, Lacona does not store any user data on its servers. We do not control nor take any responsibility for the code that runs in User Addons - please see the privacy policy or view the source code of the individual User Addon in question.

Publication of Comments

When we receive positive feedback from our users, we like to share it with others, and may use comments we've received by email, Twitter or other communication medium for the promotion of the software. We'll never show your email address, will never use your comments out of context, and will respect your privacy if you ask us not to disclose your name in association with the feedback.

Email Newsletters

We use a trusted email sender, MailChimp, to send our email newsletters, and you can find their privacy policy here.

About the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be altered and amended within reason from time to time to reflect the evolving nature of the software.

By using the Lacona App or lacona.io, you agree to our Privacy Policy above. If you have any questions about our privacy policy or terms of use, please contact us at info@lacona.io.
