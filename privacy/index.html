Lacona Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously and, when you provide us with any personal information, we endeavour to keep it safe. We will not share your personal information with third-parties and will take all reasonable precautions to protect it.

Tracking and cookies

To help us understand how we can make the Lacona website better for you, we use a Google Analytics tracking code on lacona.io. This information is anonymous and does not provide us with identifiable information.

The Lacona app tracks the number of times the application is started. Lacona Labs reserves the right to track anonymized, high-level usage metrics. Individual commands, inputs, queries, or results from using Lacona are not and will never be tracked.

Personal Data

Lacona does not store any user data on its servers. We do not control nor take any responsibility for the code that runs in Addons - please see the privacy policy or view the source code of the individual Addon in question. Purchasing and analytics are done through Paddle.

Publication of Comments

When we receive positive feedback from our users, we like to share it with others, and may use comments we've received by email, Twitter or other communication medium for the promotion of the software. We'll never show your email address, we'll never use your comments out of context, we will respect your privacy if you ask us not to disclose your name in association with the feedback.

Email Newsletters

We use a trusted email sender, MailChimp, to send our email newsletters, and you can find their privacy policy here.

About the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be altered and amended within reason from time to time to reflect the evolving nature of the software.

By using the Lacona or lacona.io, you agree to our Privacy Policy above. If you have any questions about our privacy policy or terms of use, please contact us at info@lacona.io.
